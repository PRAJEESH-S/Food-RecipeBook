Our Speciality

Tasty Burger

Burgers are prepared by using minced meat, onions, eggs and crumbs as raw materials and minced meat is an essential ingredient.

Tasty Pizza

Pizza consists of all-purpose flour, dry yeast, warm water, onion, tomato, capsicum, and different kinds of cheese along with tomato ketchup.

Cold Ice-Cream

Ice cream can be made by mixing together condensed milk, heavy cream and vanilla extract. Chill for at least 8 hours and use it in your ice cream machine.

Crispy Chicken

Dry the chicken skin thoroughly with kitchen paper - it needs to be as dry as possible. Leave to air-dry for a few minutes too, just to be sure.

Tasty Sweets

Indian sweets are usually made with a combination of several different ingredients, including sugar, ghee, flour, nuts, and spices.

Hot Coffee

The main constituents of coffee are caffeine, tannin, fixed oil, carbohydrates, and proteins. It contains 2–3% caffeine, 3–5% tannins, 13% proteins, and 10–15% fixed oils.
